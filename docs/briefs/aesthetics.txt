You are designing a website UI and visual system for a service-based business called Brazusa Cleaning, which exists under a parent brand called Brazusa.
You have already been given a full structural wireframe. Your job is to translate that structure into a cohesive, high-quality visual design system and UI implementation.
________________


CORE DESIGN OBJECTIVE
Create a design that feels:
* Clean and easy to read
* Trustworthy and professional
* Local and authentic (not corporate or franchise-like)
* Polished but not overly modern or “fake”
* Structured and intentional, but still human
Avoid extremes:
* Not noisy or cluttered
* Not overly minimal or sterile
* Not amateurish
* Not overly trendy or startup-like
________________


BRAND CONTEXT
The brand represents a fusion of Brazilian and American identity, but this should be handled subtly.
The logo already contains:
* Red, white, blue (USA)
* Yellow and green (Brazil)
* Cleaning imagery + residential + commercial elements
Because the logo is visually dense, the UI must be calm, controlled, and minimal to balance it.
________________


COLOR SYSTEM
Use a restrained, hierarchical color system.
Primary Base Colors (majority of UI)
* Deep navy: #0B2A3C (primary structural color)
* Off-white: #F7F9FB (main background)
* Light gray: #E5E9EF (section separation, borders)
Accent Colors (use intentionally, not everywhere)
* Blue: #2DAAE1 (primary CTA color)
* Yellow: #F4C430 (highlights, icons, small accents)
* Green: #4CAF50 (secondary accent, “clean/fresh” signals)
* Red: #D62828 (very minimal use only, small accents if needed)
Rules
* Do NOT use all colors equally
* Avoid large blocks of red or yellow
* Use blue for CTAs and interactive elements
* Use yellow sparingly for attention
* Use green lightly for positive/fresh cues
* Keep most of the interface navy + white + gray
________________


LAYOUT SYSTEM
* Use a medium-width layout (not ultra-wide)
* Strong vertical spacing between sections
* Clear visual separation between sections
* Avoid cramped layouts
* Avoid overly large hero sections that push content too far down
* Prioritize readability and scanning
________________


TYPOGRAPHY
Use a clean, modern, human sans-serif font.
Good examples:
* Inter
* Source Sans
* Lato
Typography should feel:
* Readable
* Slightly warm
* Not overly stylized
* Not corporate-stiff
Use clear hierarchy:
* Strong headlines
* Simple subheadings
* Highly readable body text
________________


IMAGERY
Avoid:
* Generic stock photos of smiling cleaners
* Overly staged or fake imagery
Prefer:
* Real environments
* Apartments, high-rise buildings, offices
* Subtle lifestyle imagery
* Before/after where appropriate
Images should support trust and realism.
________________


ICONOGRAPHY
* Use simple, clean line icons
* Slightly rounded style
* Consistent stroke width
* Avoid overly playful or cartoonish icons
________________


COMPONENT STYLE
Buttons
* Primary: blue background, white text
* Rounded corners (moderate, not pill-shaped)
* Clear hover states
* Strong contrast
Cards / Sections
* Light shadows or subtle borders
* Rounded corners
* Clean spacing inside
Forms
* Simple, clean inputs
* Clearly labeled
* Easy to scan
* Not overly stylized
________________


MOTION / INTERACTION
* Use minimal animation
* Smooth transitions
* Subtle hover effects
* Sticky elements should be small and unobtrusive
Avoid:
* Flashy animations
* Large intrusive popups
* Aggressive motion
________________


BRAND EXPRESSION STRATEGY
Do NOT aggressively combine Brazilian and American visuals across the UI.
Instead:
* Let the logo carry most of the identity
* Use accent colors subtly throughout
* Use tone and content to reinforce identity
* Keep the UI clean and controlled
This approach is called:
subtle cultural expression through controlled accents
________________


PAGE-SPECIFIC GUIDANCE
Brazusa Cleaning (flagship page)
* Slightly warmer and more expressive
* More imagery
* More visible accent color usage (still controlled)
* Feels personal and service-oriented
Brazusa Parent Site
* More minimal
* More structured
* Less color usage
* Feels like a clean hub
________________


UX PRIORITIES
* Fast comprehension
* Easy navigation
* Strong call-to-action visibility
* Low friction for contacting or requesting quotes
The site should feel:
* Easy to trust
* Easy to use
* Easy to act on
________________


DESIGN SUMMARY
If you had to summarize the style in one phrase:
“Clean, high-trust local service UI with minimal design and controlled brand accents”
________________


Do not deviate from these principles when generating layouts, components, or styling.
