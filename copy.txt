PRICING PHILOSOPHY
Most cleaning companies either:
   * give you a flat number with no explanation
   * or avoid pricing altogether until the last moment
We don’t like either approach.
We price based on what the job actually requires — and we’d rather explain that upfront than surprise you later.
Our goal is simple:
   * fair pricing
   * clear expectations
   * no confusion
________________


HOW WE CALCULATE PRICING
Every job is different, but pricing usually comes down to:
   * Size and layout of the space
   * Level of cleaning needed (light vs deep)
   * Type of service (routine, move-out, turnover, etc.)
   * Frequency (one-time vs recurring)
   * Accessibility (parking, building rules, timing)
   * Custom requests
We combine all of these to give a price that reflects the actual work involved.
