# 1. BRAND CORE

Brazusa is a locally owned, professionally run cleaning and operations support company based in Boston.

It combines two layers that rarely exist together in this category:

* a stable, experienced, largely Brazilian immigrant workforce with strong work ethic and low turnover
* a structured operational layer for communication, tracking, coordination, and accountability

Most providers have one of these. Brazusa is built to deliver both.

Its role is to deliver consistent, verified execution while removing as much operational burden from the client as possible.

Clients use Brazusa when they want the work handled without having to constantly check, remind, or fix things themselves.

# 4. OPERATIONAL LAYER (WHAT THIS ACTUALLY MEANS)

Brazusa operates on two layers:

## Layer 1 — Execution (Cleaning)

* Turnover cleans, maintenance cleans, deep cleans
* Consistent standards
* Stable workforce

## Layer 2 — Operational Support

* Communication and status updates
* Photo verification and reporting
* Issue detection and escalation
* Coordination across units or spaces
* Light operational tasks when structured (restocks, small fixes, access coordination)

The value is not just the cleaning itself, but that both layers work together.

Without the operational layer, cleaning becomes something the client has to manage.

With it, the service becomes predictable and easier to rely on.

# 5. TIERING MODEL (HOW WORK IS STRUCTURED)

Brazusa can operate at different levels depending on client needs.

## Base Tier — Cleaning Only

* Scheduled cleaning
* Basic communication (confirmation of completion)
* Limited flexibility

Best for:

* low complexity spaces
* clients willing to manage details themselves

---

## Managed Tier — Structured Cleaning + Communication

* Cleaning completed and confirmed
* Consistent communication
* Issues flagged proactively
* Basic coordination with client systems

Best for:

* offices
* small portfolios
* clients who want reliability without micromanagement

---

## Operational Tier — Cleaning + Integrated Support

* All of the above
* Real-time or near real-time updates
* Photo documentation and reporting
* Inventory and supply visibility support
* Coordination across multiple units
* Light operational task handling (when defined)

Best for:

* STR operators
* multi-unit clients
* clients who want to reduce daily involvement

---

## Expanded Operational Tier (Optional / Custom)

* Deeper integration into operations
* Inventory tracking and restocking support
* Linen handling coordination
* Vendor coordination (when structured)
* Customized reporting and workflows

This level requires defined scope and pricing.

---

## Important Constraint

Operational work is not unlimited.

Each engagement should define:

* what is included
* how much time is allocated
* what happens when that threshold is exceeded

This keeps the system sustainable and consistent over time.
